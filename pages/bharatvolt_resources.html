International Testing Standards

Comprehensive guide to globally recognized battery testing and certification standards for EV applications.

UN 38.3

Transportation of Dangerous Goods

Mandatory

The United Nations Manual of Tests and Criteria regulates the safe transport of lithium batteries by air, sea, rail, and road. Essential for shipping EV batteries globally.

Required Tests (8 Tests)

  • T.1 - Altitude Simulation (11.6 kPa, 6 hrs)
  • T.2 - Thermal Test (72°C & -40°C)
  • T.3 - Vibration (7-200 Hz, 12 hrs)
  • T.4 - Shock (150g, 6ms half-sine)

Additional Tests

  • T.5 - External Short Circuit (57°C)
  • T.6 - Impact/Crush (15.8mm rod, 9.1kg)
  • T.7 - Overcharge (2x recommended charge)
  • T.8 - Forced Discharge (maximum current)

Typical Timeline & Cost

Duration: 4-6 weeks | Cost: ₹2.5L - ₹8L (depending on battery size)

UL 2580

Batteries for Electric Vehicles

Recommended

UL (Underwriters Laboratories) 2580 is the North American safety standard for EV battery packs. Critical for accessing US and Canadian markets.

Electrical Safety

  • Overcharge protection
  • Short circuit tests
  • Insulation resistance

Mechanical Safety

  • Crush testing
  • Drop/impact tests
  • Vibration endurance

Environmental

  • Thermal cycling
  • Humidity exposure
  • Salt spray (corrosion)

Typical Timeline & Cost

Duration: 8-12 weeks | Cost: ₹8L - ₹25L (comprehensive testing)

IEC 62133

Secondary Cells and Batteries Safety

Mandatory EU

International standard for portable sealed secondary lithium cells and batteries. Essential for European and Asian markets. Covers both cells (Part 1) and battery packs (Part 2).

Part 1: Cell Testing

  • External short circuit (80°C)
  • Thermal abuse (130°C for 10 min)
  • Overcharge (3x nominal voltage)
  • Forced discharge (reverse charging)

Part 2: Battery Pack Testing

  • External short circuit (20°C, 55°C)
  • Moulded case stress (70°C, 7 days)
  • Dropped battery (1.9m height)
  • Overcharge (BMS protection verification)

Typical Timeline & Cost

Duration: 6-10 weeks | Cost: ₹4L - ₹12L (cells + battery pack testing)

ECE R100

REESS Safety (Electric Vehicles)

EU Mandatory

UN ECE Regulation 100 governs the approval of electric vehicles regarding the safety of Rechargeable Energy Storage Systems (REESS). Mandatory for type approval in Europe and many international markets.

Safety Requirements

  • Protection against electric shock
  • Isolation resistance (>100Ω/V DC)
  • Functional safety (ISO 26262)
  • Thermal propagation prevention

Validation Tests

  • Vibration (USCAR 2 or ISO 12405)
  • Thermal shock and cycling
  • Mechanical integrity (crash simulation)
  • Fire resistance testing

Typical Timeline & Cost

Duration: 10-16 weeks | Cost: ₹12L - ₹30L (complete vehicle REESS testing)

Indian Battery Testing Regulations

Navigate India's regulatory landscape for EV batteries. Compliance with these standards is mandatory for manufacturing and selling in India.

AIS 048 (Rev 2)

Performance of Electric Power Train

India Mandatory

Automotive Industry Standard (AIS) 048 specifies performance requirements for electric power trains in vehicles. Based on ECE R100 but with India-specific modifications.

Key Requirements

  • Battery safety & protection systems
  • Electrical safety (isolation ≥100Ω/V)
  • Functional safety validation
  • Indian climatic conditions (up to 50°C)

Testing Parameters

  • Vibration resistance (Indian roads)
  • Thermal management (-10°C to 50°C)
  • High humidity exposure (95% RH)
  • Water ingress protection (IP67)

Mandatory Compliance

Required for type approval and homologation of all electric vehicles in India. Non-compliance can result in sale prohibition.

IS 16046 (Parts 1 & 2)

Li-ion Traction Batteries for E-Vehicles

BIS Standard

Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) specification for lithium-ion traction battery packs for electric vehicles. Covers cells, modules, and complete pack assemblies.

Part 1: General Requirements

  • Electrical specifications & ratings
  • Mechanical design & construction
  • Thermal management requirements
  • Labeling & documentation

Part 2: Test Methods

  • Capacity & energy verification
  • Power capability testing
  • Cycle life evaluation
  • Safety & abuse testing

Typical Timeline & Cost

Duration: 8-14 weeks | Cost: ₹6L - ₹18L (comprehensive testing for both parts)

AIS 156 (Amendment 2)

Battery Safety for EV & Hybrid Vehicles

Critical

The most stringent Indian standard for battery safety. Mandatory for all electric and hybrid vehicles. Includes thermal propagation and fire safety requirements unique to Indian conditions.

Core Safety Tests

  • Vibration (UN R100)
  • Thermal shock & cycle
  • Mechanical integrity

Fire Safety

  • Fire resistance test
  • External fire exposure
  • Thermal propagation

Electrical Safety

  • Protection degree (IP67)
  • Overcharge protection
  • Short circuit safety

Amendment 2 Key Changes (2023)

Stricter thermal propagation requirements: 5-minute warning time mandatory. Water immersion test for IP67 rating. Enhanced BMS monitoring requirements.
